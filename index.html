About JoinSpy

JoinSpy is the ultimate security bot for Discord, protecting your community with advanced invite tracking, suspicious join detection, and a powerful global reputation system. JoinSpy helps you stop alts, spammers, and malicious accounts before they cause chaos.

JoinSpy Commands

Take full control of your server’s security with these intuitive, easy-to-use slash commands:

  • /lookup @user — Get detailed info on when and how a user joined your server, including inviter, reputation, and account age.
  • /globalrep @user — View a user’s global reputation history across all servers using JoinSpy, including suspicious join patterns and lowest reputation recorded.
  • /setlogs #channel — Define where JoinSpy should send logs for member joins, leaves, suspicious activity, and watchlist alerts.
  • /stats — Access server-specific statistics, including join counts, suspicious joins, and average account age of members.
  • /help — Launch an interactive help menu that guides staff through all available features and commands.

Commands are designed with moderators in mind — providing instant insights to take action quickly and efficiently.
